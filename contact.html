Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?+
We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for weddings and pre-wedding shoots, and 1–2 weeks for birthdays and smaller celebrations, to ensure your preferred date and photographer.
Do you travel outside Delhi NCR?+
Yes! We regularly shoot destination pre-weddings and weddings across India. Travel and accommodation charges apply separately based on location.
How long until we receive our photos?+
Delivery timelines depend on your package — typically 5 to 10 days for edited photos, with a sneak peek shared within 48 hours of your shoot.
Can we customize a package?+
Absolutely. Every celebration is different, so we're happy to tailor coverage hours, number of photographers, albums, and films to suit your needs and budget.
What's your payment policy?+
We require a 30% advance to confirm your booking, with the balance due on or before the event day. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and cash.
